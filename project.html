About Movie

Did you know that?

  1. For the filming of the film, Cameron rented the MBS Media Campus set in Southern California for a period of 5 years. Characteristic features of this site are an "aggressive" water conservation program, the use of environmentally friendly cleaning products and paints, as well as a number of other "green" programs. Cameron intends to install solar panels in the studio and shoot both films using solar energy. The cost of installing the appropriate equipment will cost the director € 4.4 million.
  2. In March 2012, aboard the Deepsea Challenger submersible, James Cameron dived to the bottom of the Mariana Trench to seek inspiration for Pandora's ocean floor and a 3D documentary about the Pacific Trench. Thus, Cameron became the third person in history to undertake such an expedition.
